Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 5, 2026

BrainBlox ("the App") is a free-to-play mobile game developed by an independent developer. This privacy policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

Information We Collect

All game data (level progress, settings, and purchase status) is stored locally on your device and is never sent to any server. We do not collect any personally identifiable information.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (based on IP)
  • App usage data

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit Google's partner policy page.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising through their device settings. Users may also purchase an in-app upgrade to permanently remove all advertisements.

In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional one-time in-app purchase to remove advertisements. This transaction is processed entirely by Google Play. The developer does not collect or have access to any payment information.

Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App contains third-party advertising which may collect data as described above.

Analytics

The App uses Google Firebase Analytics (Google Analytics for Firebase) to collect anonymous usage data. This helps us understand how players use the App and improve the experience. Data collected includes:

  • App interactions (screens viewed, features used, session duration)
  • Device information (device model, OS version)
  • Device identifiers
  • Crash and performance data

This data is anonymous and aggregated. It is not used to identify individual users. For more information, see Firebase Privacy.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob — advertising
  • Google Firebase Analytics — analytics
  • Google Play Billing — in-app purchases

Data Retention

The App does not store any data on external servers. All game data is stored locally on your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's data through your device settings.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
